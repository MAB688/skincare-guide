General Skin Care Routine

This skincare routine can be followed in the morning when you wake up or at night before bed. It is recommended that you follow this twice a day, once in the morning and once at night.


1 - Cleanser

When using cleanser, you want to be very gentle as to not irritate the skin which means you should not use anything but your clean hands. Follow these simple steps:
1. Wet your face with warm water.
2. Gently rub cleanser into your skin while being sure to not irritate your skin.
3. Once you have thoroughly cleaned your skin, rinse your face with warm water.
4. Finally, use a soft cloth and pat dry your skin.


Step 2 - Serum

After successfully cleansing your face, the next step in your skincare routine is serum. Follow these simple steps:
1. Pump a small amount of serum in your hands and rub them together.
2. Gently apply the serum to your skin in a circular motion and spend a little extra time in particularly problematic spots.
3. Once you are done, allow the serum to dry. Do not rinse your skin or wipe it off.


3 - Moisturizer

Once the serum is dry, you can pump a good amount of moisturizer and gently rub it in to your skin. That's it, you have completed your skincare routine!
